💖 Our Story

Born in the lanes
of Hussainabad

Mumbai started as a small cart in 2018 with just three flavours and a deep love for the craft. Today, we're a Lucknow favourite — but the recipe for joy hasn't changed one bit.

🌸
Made fresh, every morning
We churn small batches before sunrise so every scoop you get is the day's best.
🥭
Real fruit, no shortcuts
No artificial flavours. We source seasonal fruits directly — Devgad alphonso, Mahabaleshwar strawberry, Kashmiri saffron.
🌿
Rooted in Lucknow's warmth
The spirit of nawabi hospitality lives in every bowl — generous portions, genuine warmth, fair price.
